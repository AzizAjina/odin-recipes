Curry

Delicious Japanese chicken curry recipe for a weeknight dinner!

Tender pieces of chicken, carrots, and potatoes cooked in a rich savory curry sauce, this Japanese version of curry is a must-have for your family meal.

Curry Rice (Kare Raisu, カレーライス) is an exemplary comfort food and one of the most-loved home-cooked dishes in Japan.

Ingredients

  • Jasmine / Basmati rice
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Chicken breats
  • Curry powder
  • Potatoes
  • oil

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Cut the onions in half and cut each half into 5 wedges.
  3. Peel the carrots and cut into bite-sized pieces.
  4. Peel the potatoes and cut them into quarters. Soak them in water to remove the starch.
  5. Cut the meat into bite-sized pieces
  6. Put oil in the pot
  7. Then, add the sliced onions
  8. Add the chicken to the pot and mix until just coated with oil
  9. Add the carrots and potatoes to the pot and mix well
  10. Add the chicken broth and use a spatula to press down the meat and vegetables into the liquid. Then, place the curry roux blocks
  11. Make the rice
  12. Serve on individual plates over steamed Japanese rice
